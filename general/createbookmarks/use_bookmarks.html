Now that we have spatial bookmarks, we can quickly zoom to the bookmarks' locations.

In the Spatial Bookmarks Panel, do:

  1. Click the Raleigh bookmark line.
  2. Click the Zoom to bookmark

The map canvas should zoom to the previously saved extention around Raleigh.

Try reapeating the above steps but for the the Wake county bookmark. After doing it, the map canvas should now show the Wake county full territory.

Once you are done, click Move to next step to finish the lesson.
