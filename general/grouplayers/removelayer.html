You can also remove layers from the group by drag n' drop.

Let's remove the Wake_Public_Schools from the group. In the Layers Panel:

  1. Click over the Wake_Public_Schools layer and hold the mouse button to grab it.
  2. Move it to be over the Wake_Boundary layer, but slightly above it (note the longer line that shows up), and release the mouse button.

The Wake_Public_Schools layer should now be outside the Services group.

Once you have finished, click Move to next step to end the lesson.
