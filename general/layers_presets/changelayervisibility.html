Now, that we have the current layers visibility saved, let's create a new combination. In the Layers Panel:

  1. Disable the checkboxes next to Wake_EMS_Station, Wake_Fire_Station, Wake_Police_Station, and Wake_Public_schools layers to turn their visibility off.
  2. Enable the checkbox next to Services heatmap layer to turn its visibility on.

Once you have finished, click Move to next step.
