In the Coordinate Reference System Selector dialog:

  1. Type North Carolina in the Filter field.
  2. Choose the NAD83/North Carolina (ftUS) | EPSG:2264 in the Coordinate Reference Systems of the world list.
  3. Click OK to apply and close the dialog

The new CRS is set, and the layer should now fit the Wake County's Boundary.

Once you have finished, click Next step.
