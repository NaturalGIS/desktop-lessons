In the Scale Bar Decoration dialog, do the following:

  1. Click the checkbox to Enable scale bar.
  2. Set Scale bar style to Tick Up
  3. Keep Color of bar as Black
  4. Keep Size of bar as 30 meters/km
  5. Keep Automatically snap to round number on resize enabled
  6. Set Placement to Bottom right.
  7. Keep Margin to edge options
  8. Click OK to apply the settings and close the dialog

A scale bar should appear at the Bottom right corner of the map canvas.
