We will start by selecting the layer we want to group. In the Layers Panel, do:

  1. Hold Ctrl key (Cmd on OSX).
  2. Click over Wake_Police_Stations, Wake_EMS_Stations, Wake_Fire_Station, and Wake_Public_Schools layers to make them active.
  3. Release the Ctrl key (Cmd on OSX).

The mentioned layers should look highlighted.

Once you have finished, click Next step.
