You can add layers to an already existing group by drag n' drop them.

We forgot to add the Services heatmap layer to the group. Let's add it. In the Layers Panel:

  1. Click over the Services heatmap layer and hold the mouse button to grab it.
  2. Move it to be over the Services group's last layer (Wake_Public_Schools) but slightly down (note the shorter line that shows up), and release the mouse button.

The Services heatmap layer should now be inside the Services group.

Once you have finished, click Next step.
